Who We Are

Founded to empower the manufacturing sector, Arise CNC Tools specializes in the design, development, and supply of high-performance cutting tools, tool holding systems, and machining accessories.

With decades of combined expertise, we deliver reliable solutions for CNC milling, drilling, boring, and turning across automotive, aerospace, die & mold, and precision engineering.

Our Mission

To deliver superior CNC tooling solutions that enhance productivity, accuracy, and tool life — while building long-term partnerships through trust, service, and continuous innovation.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a global leader in CNC tooling technology — where precision meets performance and innovation drives every cut.

Our Expertise

End-to-end tooling for milling, drilling, boring, and turning

Milling Tools

Face mills, end mills, profiling cutters for steel, aluminium, cast iron.

  • High-balance bodies & advanced coatings
  • Geometry options for roughing & finishing
Drilling Tools

Solid carbide drills, indexable U drills for high penetration rates.

  • Through-coolant options
  • Optimized point & flute geometry
Boring & Reaming

Precision boring bars, damped holders, and carbide/CBN reamers.

  • µm-level adjustability
  • Vibration control for superior finishes
Turning & Threading

General turning, parting & grooving, threading, multi-task tools.

  • Stable clamping & chip control
  • Grades for steel, SS, CI, non-ferrous
Tool Holding Systems

CAT/BT/HSK adapters, ER collets, hydraulic & damped holders.

  • High runout accuracy < 3 µm
  • Extended reach & anti-vibration options
Inserts

Carbide, PCD, and CBN inserts tuned to your material & operation.

  • Wear-resistant grades & chipbreakers
  • ISO/ANSI geometries available
