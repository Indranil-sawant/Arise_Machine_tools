Industries We Serve

Arise CNC Tools delivers high-performance tooling solutions across a diverse range of sectors. From aerospace precision to heavy engineering durability, our tools are engineered to meet the specific demands of every industry.

Automotive

High-speed machining solutions for engine components, transmission parts, and chassis manufacturing.

  • Engine Block Machining
  • Transmission Gears
  • Brake System Components

Aerospace

Precision tools for exotic materials like Titanium and Inconel, ensuring safety and reliability in flight.

  • Turbine Blade Finishing
  • Structural Components
  • Landing Gear Parts

Medical

Micro-machining capabilities for surgical instruments, implants, and orthopedic devices with superior finish.

  • Surgical Implants
  • Dental Prosthetics
  • Bone Screws & Plates

Die & Mold

Hard milling solutions for complex 3D contours and hardened steel molds with extended tool life.

  • Injection Molds
  • Die Casting Dies
  • Stamping Tools

Energy & Power

Heavy-duty tooling for wind turbines, oil & gas components, and power generation equipment.

  • Wind Turbine Hubs
  • Oil & Gas Valves
  • Turbine Rotors

General Engineering

Versatile tooling solutions for job shops and contract manufacturers handling diverse materials.

  • Custom Fixtures
  • Machine Parts
  • Prototype Development
